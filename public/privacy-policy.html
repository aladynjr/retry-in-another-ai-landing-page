Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how "Retry in Another AI" ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

Our extension is designed to operate with minimal data collection. We only process the following information:

  • Conversation content from ChatGPT and Claude that you explicitly choose to transfer
  • Temporary storage of conversation data in Chrome's local storage during the transfer process

How We Handle Your Data

  • All data processing occurs locally within your browser
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Conversation data is temporarily stored in Chrome's local storage only during the transfer process
  • Data is automatically cleared from local storage after the transfer is complete

Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) to temporarily store conversation data during transfers. This data is:

  • Stored only on your local device
  • Automatically deleted after successful transfer
  • Never persisted beyond the transfer process
  • Not synced across devices

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • Access to chat.openai.com and claude.ai - to facilitate conversation transfers
  • Storage permission - to temporarily store conversation data during transfers

Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with:

  • ChatGPT (chat.openai.com)
  • Claude (claude.ai)

Please note that these services have their own privacy policies and terms of service that govern your use of their platforms.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data processing occurs locally in your browser
  • No external network requests are made with your conversation data
  • Temporary data is automatically cleared after use

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear your browser's local storage
  • Choose which conversations to transfer
